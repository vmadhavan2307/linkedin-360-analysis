Three Analysis Frameworks

Universal Mode

Best for: Understanding your overall strengths and positioning.

Analyzes: 7 core dimensions (intellectual depth, execution, technical mastery, customer obsession, cross-functional leadership, people development, personal excellence)

Output: Dimension ratings with evidence, career arc, role positioning

Executive Mode

Best for: VP/Director interviews and executive positioning.

Analyzes: 9 dimensions across 3 tiers (Strategic Leadership, People Leadership, Foundational Excellence)

Output: Tier scorecard, interview emphasis points, executive readiness assessment

Reforge PM Mode

Best for: Product managers targeting Senior PM or PM Manager roles.

Analyzes: 8 PM competencies (strategy, execution, data, user research, design, technical, cross-functional, communication)

Output: PM competency ratings, archetype analysis, stage readiness

FAQ

Q: Do I need a Claude API key?
A: No. Use claude.ai (web interface) for free. If you have Pro, it's included. API users can use their own key if they have one.
Q: How many recommendations do I need?
A: 10+ recommended for meaningful patterns. Works with as few as 5, but signal is clearer with more. 20+ recommendations give the strongest analysis.
Q: Is my data safe?
A: Completely. Your data only goes to Claude (Anthropic's API). It doesn't touch this site, my servers, or any third party. No tracking, no data collection.
Q: Can I use this for other feedback types?
A: Yes. The framework is modular. You can adapt it for peer reviews, customer testimonials, 360 feedback, or any multi-source feedback analysis.
Q: How long does analysis take?
A: 30–60 seconds depending on recommendation length and Claude's response time.
Q: Can I customize the frameworks?
A: Absolutely. The skill is open-source. You can modify dimensions, add new frameworks, or adapt for your use case.
Q: Can I offer this as a service or charge for it?
A: Yes. The skill is MIT licensed. You can run analyses for others, generate HTML outputs, and offer it as a coaching/recruitment service.
